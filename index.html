Our story

A tiny café with a big heart

抹茶

We're a family-owned coffee studio in Burnaby inspired by the quiet craft of Japanese coffee culture. Every drink is made slowly, intentionally, and with genuine care.

What started as a pop-up in 2011 became a permanent neighbourhood fixture in 2017. Our small space — just a handful of seats — means every visit feels personal. We know our regulars by name.

We source locally where we can and brew everything to order. No shortcuts.
